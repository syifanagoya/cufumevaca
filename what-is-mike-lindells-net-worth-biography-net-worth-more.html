Who is Mike Lindell?

Mike was known for jumping from moving automobiles, getting stuck beneath an ice sheet after falling into a frozen lake, crashing two motorbikes, getting electrocuted, and skydiving with a half-opened parachute. Lindell enrolled at the University of Minnesota, but his studies were cut short. He learned to count cards while working as a part-time bartender after dropping out of university.During his twenties, Mike got into cocaine addiction in the 1980s. He needed a harder substance to feed his addiction, so he moved to crack cocaine in the 1990s.

His years of drug usage were coupled with his gambling habit, which resulted in the foreclosure of his house and his wife filing for divorce. Mike was addicted to multiple narcotics, owing tens of thousands of dollars to bookies, and had a history of DUIs and thievery.
