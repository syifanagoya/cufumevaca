VIDEO Keeping Up With The Kardashians Season 9 preview trailer

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, March 21, 2024

Season 9 of Keeping Up With the Kardashians premieres Sunday night at 9/8c and E! has just released the first extended preview trailer that suggests this may be the most drama-packed season yet!

So let’s just run through a little checklist of things we can expect on this season of KUWTK, shall we?

• Kim and Kanye’s wedding and all the preparation leading up to it

• The Kardashians in Thailand

• Khloe opening up about her split with Lamar

• More on Kris Jenner and Bruce Jenner’s split (and possible reconciliation?)

• Kourtney Kardashian and Scott Disick’s most recent pregnancy

• The headline-grabbing night in Vienna when Kim Kardashian and her family were approached by a white man in blackface and were later part of an interview where another person casually used the “n” word

• The $300,000 jewelry and cash theft from Khloe and Kourtney that was believed to have been somehow connected to a show crew member(s)

• The sale of Khloe and Lamar’s home

• Kendall Jenner grows up, starts becoming a super celeb on her own

• Khloe’s new man French Montana

• Momager drama

• LOTS of Jenners and Kardashians in bikinis

In other words, Season 9 of Keeping Up With the Kardashians is basically a documented recap of 63% of all the tabloid headlines for the last four months. Say what you will (haters gonna hate), but that sounds like some damned entertaining television!
