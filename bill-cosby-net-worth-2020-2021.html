At school, he used to be the captain of both track and field and baseball teams.

Cosby has done several jobs during his college life and after college life among them being a shoe shiner, apprentice, selling producer, stocking shelves, bartending and even as Hospital Corpsman.

Irrespective of the numerous sex assault charges facing Cosby don’t get it wrong he is married to Camille Olivia Hanks where they exchanged vows in 1964. Together the couple had five children, but today they have four since one was murdered in 1997.
