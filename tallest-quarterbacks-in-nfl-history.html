15 Tallest Quarterbacks in NFL History

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, February 22, 2024

Who are the 15 tallest quarterbacks in NFL history?

Quarterbacks are the backbone in NFL.

From examining the defenses to calling the plays and throwing a wonderful spiral to running with the ball and leading the teams to victory with touchdowns, quarterbacks are the most precious treasures of the NFL.

And for winning the championships and making history, a quarterback needs to earn loads of passing yards, touchdown passes, and a high percentage of pass completions during each season.

Most of the elite quarterbacks in the NFL have incredible speed, accuracy, excellent arm strength, powerful handgrip, and smooth and consistent play movements, which all correlate to their tall heights.

With their tall stature, it is easy to look over the defense unit and overall gameplay.

Whereas their long and big hands and bulky arms give them the advantage of better ball control, making their throws reach higher and strike in the sky.

Further, it is a massive deal for the NFL to find a great quarterback who must at least be 6’3″ to enter the league.

With all that said, let’s take a quick look at the 15 tallest quarterbacks in NFL history.

15. Tony Pike

Born: 10th March 1986 (36 years)

Height: 6 ft 6 inches

Team: Carolina Panthers

Roster Status: Retired

Standing at 6 ft 6 inches, Tony Pike is one of the tallest quarterbacks in NFL history.

Pike made his NFL debut in 2010 when the Carolina Panthers selected him as the 204th pick in the sixth round of the NFL Draft.

He played for a single season with the Panthers.

During his time with the Panthers, Pike had a record of 47 yards passed with a passer rating of 60.1%

Since retiring, he has been serving as the commentator for ESPN 1530 and the Cincinnati Bearcats football team.

14. Jonathan Quinn

Born: 27th February 1975 (47 years)

Height: 6 ft 7 inches

Team: Jacksonville Jaguars, Kansas City Chiefs, and Chicago Bears

Roster Status: Retired

Coming to the 14th spot is another 6 ft 6 inches tall former NFL quarterback.

In 1998, the Jacksonville Jaguars selected Jonathan Quinn as the 86th pick in the third round of the NFL Draft.

Besides, he played for seven seasons in NFL with the Jaguars, Kansas City Chiefs, and Chicago Bears.

In 2001, Quinn led the Berlin Thunder to win World Bowl IX Championship by passing on 2,247 yards. Besides, he was honored as the World Bowl IX MVP.

During his time on the field, Quinn had a record of 4 touchdowns and seven interceptions on 1,161 passing yards with a passing rate of 60.4%.

13. JaMarcus Russell

Born: 9th August 1985 (36 years)

He had a career record of 18 touchdowns and 23 interceptions on 4,083 passing yards with a passing rate of 65.2%.

12. Matt Schaub

Born: 25th June 1981 (40 years)

Height: 6 ft 6 inches

Team: Atlanta Falcons, Houston Texans, and Baltimore Ravens

Roster Status: Retired

Matt Schaub, standing 6 ft 6 inches tall, is a former NFL quarterback from Pennsylvania.

In 2004, the Atlanta Falcons selected him as the 90th pick in the third round of the NFL Draft.

He played for 17 seasons in NFL with the teams like Falcons, Houston Texans, and Baltimore Ravens.

With Texans, he made two Pro Bowls appearances and led the team in passing yards in 2009.

During his time on the field, Schaub had earned 136 touchdowns and 91 interceptions in 25, 467 passing yards with a passing rate of 89.7%.

11. John Skelton

Born: 17th March 1988 (34 years)

Height: 6 ft 6 inches

Team: Arizona Cardinals, Cincinnati Bengals, San Francisco 49ers, and Tennessee Titans

Roster Status: Retired

The 11th tallest quarterback in NFL history is John Skelton, who stands 6 ft 6 inches tall.

10. Nate Sudfeld

Born: 7th October 1993 (28 years)

Height: 6 ft 6 inches

Team: San Francisco 49ers (current)

Roster Status: Active

Nate Sudfeld, standing 6 ft 6 inches tall, is a quarterback in NFL’s San Francisco 49ers.

Sudfeld made his NFL debut in 2016 when the Washington Redskins selected him as the 108th pick in the sixth round of the NFL Draft.

In 2017, the Philadelphia Eagles signed him as a backup quarterback. With them, Sudfeld won Super Bowl LII during the first season.

Besides, he played with the Eagles till 2020.

To date, Sudfeld has earned an interception in 188 passing yards and has a passing rate of 77.3%.

8. Marc Wilson

6. Sonny Gibbs

Born: 25th October 1939 (82 years)

Height: 6ft 7 inches

Roster Status: Retired

4. Paxton Lynch

Born: 12th February 1994 (28 years)

Height: 6 ft 7 inches

Roster Status: Retired

Standing 6 ft 7 inches tall, Paxton Lynch is the fourth tallest quarterback in NFL history.

3. Brock Osweiler

Born: 22nd November 1990 (31 years)

Height: 6ft 7 inches

2. Frank Patrick

Born: 11th March 1947 (75 years)

1. Dan McGwire

Born: 18th December 1967 (54 years)

Height: 6ft 8 inches

Roster Status: Retired

And the tallest NFL quarterback to ever exist is Dan McGwire, who stands 6 ft 8 inches tall.
