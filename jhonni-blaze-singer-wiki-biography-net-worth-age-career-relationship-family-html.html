    Professional Life

    Jhonni Blaze began her career as a stripper and sex worker in Houston. Through her profession, she built connections in the modeling industry and started working as a video vixen. She appeared in numerous music videos with renowned artists such as Chris Brown, Young Berg, French Montana, Jim Jones, and Jadakiss, among others.

    In 2015, Jhonni gained international recognition when she made her debut on the reality TV show “Love & Hip Hop: New York” Season 5, which aired on VHThe show featured other personalities like Precious Paris, Rich Dollaz, and Diamond Strawberry. Jhonni Blaze was also approached to join the cast of the spin-off “Love & Hip Hop: Houston,” but the show did not materialize due to escalating violence, including a fight between Jhonni and Just Brittany.

    Jhonni Blaze has since appeared on other reality shows such as “Dirty Little Secrets,” “Love & Hip Hop: The Love Edition,” “Love & Hip Hop: Atlanta,” “Black Ink Crew: Chicago,” and “Growing Up Hip Hop: Atlanta.” Her face has graced the pages of top magazines like Hip Hop Weekly, King, XXL, and Black Men’s Magazine. Jhonni Blaze continues to collaborate with Rich Dollaz.

    Singing Career

    Jhonni Blaze ventured into the music industry by starting a YouTube channel called “The Jhonni Blaze.” Her first video, posted on October 7, 2014, aimed to provide glimpses into her life. Over time, she began sharing song covers and original tracks on the platform. One of her most successful songs, “Jhonni Blaze Ft. Ghetto Barbie – Want The Money,” released on August 9, 2018, garnered over 2.9 million views within a few hours. Her track “Where You From” ft. Elephant Man, released on November 23, 2018, has accumulated over 7 million views.
