Eric Gordon Basketball Career

Gordon, who was picked seventh overall by the Los Angeles Clippers in 2008, is in his 16th NBA season. He was a member of the Phoenix Suns’ Big 3 in 2021, along with Chris Paul and Devin Booker.

Eric Gordon’s Defensive Ability

Eric Gordon’s age and height haven’t stopped him from defending himself. His defensive importance is highlighted by his mobility, as well as 1.1 steals and 0.6 blocks per game in the 2022-2023 season. Furthermore, his status as the NBA’s 10th-best in defensive real plus-minus (DRPM) speaks much about his defensive impact.
